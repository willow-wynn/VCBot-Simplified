A BILL
To appropriate money in case of a government shutdown in June.
Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE
(a) SHORT TITLE — This Act may be cited as the “Secure
Appropriations For Everyone Act of June 2021” or abbreviated to
the “S.A.F.E. Act of June 2021”
SECTION 2. FINDINGS
(a) FINDINGS — Congress finds that the government shutdown is a
very real threat due to the debate and reconciliation process of
budget-writing and amending.
SECTION 3. RENAMING

Page 2
(a) IN GENERAL — There are appropriated, monies, in such ratios as
prescribed by the Complete and Healthy Appropriations Now Act of
May 2021, for ninety-six hours past midnight on June 1st, 2021, to
prevent a government shutdown.
(b) FUNDING — The amounts appropriated for ninety-six hours shall
be $53,751,634,466.10, to be apportioned under Subsection (c) of
this Section.
(c) RATIOS — The monies appropriated above shall be appropriated to
each agency and entity by ratio of up to but no more than
four-thirtieths of such funds appropriated in the Complete and
Healthy Appropriations Now Act of May 2021.
(d) VOIDING — This act shall be repealed if either —
(i) The calendar date in Washington, D.C. is June 5th.
(ii) An appropriations act is passed.
SECTION 4. ENACTMENT
(a) ENACTMENT — The provisions of this Act shall come into force on
May 31st, 2021.
SECTION 5. SEVERABILITY
(a) SEVERABILITY — Should any provision of this Act be deemed
invalid or unconstitutional for any reason in a court with relevant
jurisdiction, the rest of the Act, and the application of the
remaining provisions, shall not be affected.
