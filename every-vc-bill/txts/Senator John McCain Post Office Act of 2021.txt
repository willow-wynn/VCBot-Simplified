Mr. REYES-GUERRERO-JUSTICE (For himself, Mr. SULLIVAN, Mr. NOAH,

A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal
Service located at 3905 N. 7th Ave. in Phoenix, Arizona as
the “Senator John McCain Post Office Building”
1 Be it enacted by the Senate and House of
2 Representatives of the United States of America in Congress
3 assembled,
4 SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
5 (a) SHORT TITLE—This Act may be cited as the
6 “Senator John McCain Post Office Act of 2021”

2
1 SECTION 2. SEVERABILITY
2 (a) SEVERABILITY—If any provision of this Act, an
3 amendment made by this Act, or the application of such
4 provision or amendment to any person or circumstance is
5 held to be unconstitutional, the remainder of this Act, the
6 amendments made by this Act, and the application of the
7 provisions of such to any person or circumstance shall not
8 be affected thereby.
9 SECTION 3. FINDINGS.
10 Congress finds and declares the following—
11 (a) John McCain served honorably in the United States
12 Armed Forces in Vietnam, where he was imprisoned and
13 became a prisoner of war;
14 (b) John McCain served honorably in the House of
15 Representatives and the Senate, passing historic
16 legislation;
17 (c) John McCain was an influential Congressional
18 figure and continues to inspire Congress and the American
19 people through his legacy.
20 SECTION 4. SENATOR JOHN MCCAIN POST
21 OFFICE BUILDING.
22 (a) DESIGNATION—The facility of the United States
23 Postal Service located at 3905 N. 7th Ave. in Phoenix,
24 Arizona, shall be known and designated as the “Senator
25 John McCain Post Office Building”
26 (b) REFERENCES—Any reference in a law, map,
27 regulation, document, paper, or other record of the United
28 States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be

3
1 deemed to be a reference to the “Senator John McCain Post
2 Office Building”
3 SECTION 5. ENACTMENT.
4 (a) The provisions of this Act shall go into effect
5 immediately upon its passage into law.
