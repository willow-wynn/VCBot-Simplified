A BILL
To make grants available to the following organizations, Girl Scouts and Boy
Scouts of America, the National 4-H Council, and the National FFA
Organization to create pilot projects to expand programs currently carried out by
such organizations in rural areas and small towns, and for other purposes.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
America in Congress assembled,
