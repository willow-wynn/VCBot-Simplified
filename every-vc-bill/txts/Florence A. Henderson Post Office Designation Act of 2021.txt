A BILL
To designate the facility of the United StatesPostalService locatedat4E Medcalf Street
in Dale,Lincoln (Indiana), asthe “FlorenceA. HendersonPostOffice”
Beit enactedbythe Senateandthe House ofRepresentativesof theUnited Statesin
Congress assembled,
SECTION 1.FlorenceA. Henderson PostOffice
(a) Delegation.—
The facility of the United States Postal Service located at 4 E Medcalf Street in
Dale, Lincoln (Indiana), as the “Florence A. Henderson Post Office”.
(b) References.—

Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other record of the
United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a
reference to the “Florence A. Henderson Post Office”.
