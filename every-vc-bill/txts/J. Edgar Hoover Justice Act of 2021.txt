H.R. 4
IN THE 142nd CONGRESS OF THE UNITED STATES
June 21, 2021
Representative Purdy (for himself, Representative Cage,
Representative Cream Soda, Senator Miller,) introduced the
following;
An Act
To remove Edgar Hoover’s name from the J. Edgar Hoover Federal Building and for other
purposes
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in
Congress assembled,
Section 1. Short Title
(a) This Act may be cited as the “J. Edgar Hoover Justice Act”.
Sec. 2. Redesignation
(a) The Federal building located at 935 Pennsylvania Avenue Northwest in the
District of Columbia and is commonly known as the J. Edgar Hoover Federal
Building shall be known and designated as the “FBI Federal Building.”
(b) Pennsylvania Avenue Northwest within the territory of the District of
Columbia shall be known and designated as “Civil Rights Avenue.”
Sec. 3. References
(a) All references in a paper, document, regulation, map, law, or other record of
the United States to the Federal building referred to in section 2 shall be
deemed to be a reference to the “FBI Federal Building.”

Sec. 4. Supremacy
(a) For any provision of any statute that contradicts any section of this act, the
contradictory provision of that statute shall be struck and nullified.
Sec. 5. Enactment
(a) The provisions of this Act shall come into force immediately upon passage.
