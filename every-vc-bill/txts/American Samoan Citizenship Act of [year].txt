The American Samoan Citizenship Act
Whereas The Congress sees it fit to grant all People Living on American Samoa United States
Citizens and all People born on American Samoa.
Section 1
a. All American Nationals who are currently living in American Samoa shall become United
States citizens effective Two months after this act shall be signed into law by the
President.
Section 2
a. All Persons currently living on American Samoa and use U.S. Passports designating
them as “Nationals” are entitled to exchange their passport for an updated one for no
additional cost to themselves.
