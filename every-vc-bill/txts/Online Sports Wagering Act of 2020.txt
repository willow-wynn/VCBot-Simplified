IN THE CONGRESS OF THE UNITED STATES
JANUARY 18, 2020
Mr. SORO (for himself, Mr. TARKIN, Mr. MCDONOUGH, Mr. HAGEN, Mr.
HARRIS, and Mr. VITA) introduced the following bill;
A BILL
To formally legalize the act of wagering on sporting events over the Internet.
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the ”Online Sports Wagering Act of 2020”.
SEC. 2. FINDINGS.
(1) That in Murphy v National Collegiate Athletic Association the Supreme Court
found certain restrictions of in-person sports wagering unconstitutional.
(2)That the Unlawful Internet Gambling Enforcement Act of 2006 already excludes
wagering on fantasy sporting events.
SEC. 3.REMOVAL IN STATUTE.
(a) In General. — The Unlawful Internet Gambling Enforcement Act (31 U.S.C. §§
5361–5367) is amended—
(1) in subchapter 5362 (31 U.S.C. § 5362)—
(A) in paragraph (1)—
(i) in subparagraph (A)—
(I) by striking, “contest” and “sports event”
(2) In General. —The Interstate Wire Act (18 U.S.C § 1084) is amended—
(1) in paragraph (a)—
(A) by striking, “sporting event” and “contest”
SEC. 4. ENACTMENT.
This Act shall go into effect on the date which it is adopted by the Congress.
SEC. 5. CONFLICTIONS.
All laws in conflict with this legislation are hereby declared null and void.
