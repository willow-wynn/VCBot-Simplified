SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the Federal Vacancy Reform Act of 2021 of April
2021 ”
SEC. 2. AMENDING THE MAXIMUM TENURE OF ACTING
SECRETARIES
(a) IN GENERAL — 5 U.S. Code § 3346 is amended as follows
(i) All instances of ”210” shall be stricken and replaced with “12”
(ii) All instances of “Senate” are to be replaced with “House of
Representatives”.
(b) EXCEPTIONS — Any Current Acting Officers shall be exempt from this
section for a period of 10 days following the passage of this Act.

SEC. 3. TECHNICAL AMENDMENTS
(a) IN GENERAL.— 5 U.S.C.§3347-3349d shall be amended as follows
(i) All instances of “Senate” shall be stricken and replaced with “House of
Representatives”.
(ii) All instances of “210 days” in these same sections shall be replaced
with “12 days”.
(b) VACANCIES DURING TRANSITION — 5 U.S. Code § 3349a shall be
amended as follows
(i) In Section(b)(1)
(1) “90” shall be stricken and replaced with “10”
(ii) In Section(b)(2)
(1) “90” shall be stricken and replaced with “10”
SEC. 4. ENACTMENT.
EFFECTIVE DATE.— The provisions of this Act shall come into force
immediately upon passage.
