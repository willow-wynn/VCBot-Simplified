A BILL
Toallowfor theadmissionof theCommonwealth ofPuerto Ricointo theState ofJackson.
1

Beit enactedbytheSenateandHouse ofRepresentativesof theUnited StatesofAmericain
Congressassembled,
Section 1. Short Title
A. This Actmaybe citedas the“PuertoRicoAdmissionAct”.
SEC. 2 - Findings
A. On August12,2022,the SecretaryofState delivered aletter toCongress andtheJackson
Assemblystating thatit wastheintention ofthe citizensof theCommonwealth of Puerto
Ricoto join theStateof Jacksonviareferendum. Uponfurther communicationwith the
Puerto Ricangovernment, itwasfound that theresults ofthis referendum wereindeed
valid.
SEC. 3 - Statement of Policy
A. Whereas it wasthedeclared intentionof thepeople ofPuerto Ricotojoin theState of
Jackson,this billwould authorizethe State ofJacksontoincorporate theentire
Commonwealth ofPuerto Ricointo theState under theConstitutionof theUnited States
and theUnitedStatesCode.
SEC. 4 - Authorization
A. Pursuantto Article IV,Section 3,Clause1of theConstitutionof theUnited States,the
State of Jacksonis authorized toincorporatethe Commonwealthof Puerto Ricoinits
entirety.
SEC. 5 - Equal Footing Clause
A. Pursuantto theEqualFootingDoctrine asstatedin thedecisionof Pollard’sLesseev.
Hagan,44U.S. (3 How.) 212(1845), theState ofJacksonis required toadmit the
Commonwealth ofPuerto Ricoonequal footing asall currentlyexistingterritories within
theState.
SEC. 6 - Conflicting Laws
A. All lawsthatconflictwith thisone areherebynullandvoid.
SEC. 7 - WHEN EFFECTIVE
A. This Actshalltakeeffect immediatelyuponpassage.
