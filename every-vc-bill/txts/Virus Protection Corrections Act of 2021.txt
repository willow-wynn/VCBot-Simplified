A BILL
Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the United States in
America assembled,
SECTION 1. TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the “Virus Protection Corrections Act”.
SECTION 2. FINDINGS.
Congress finds that—
(a) The definition of the term “authorized” is “To empoweranother with the legal right to
perform an action.”.
(b) The definition of the term “authorized” is “to designate by the government or an
individual of the use to which a fund of money is to be applied.”.
(c) An authorization of funds towards a cause by Congressdoes not provide new funds to
such relevant causes.
SECTION 3. MINOR LEGISLATIVE AMENDMENT.
(a) TheVirus Prevention Act of 2021 shall be amended—

(i) in Section 6 by striking “APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be
authorized, under the jurisdiction of the Health andHuman Services
Department, for the programs established within thisSection,
$1,000,000,000 for combined Fiscal Months Februaryto December
2021.” and replacing such text in lieu with
“APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be appropriated, underthe
jurisdiction of the Health and Human Services Department,for the
programs established within this Section, $1,000,000,000for combined
Fiscal Months February to December 2021.”
(ii) in Section 7 by striking “APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be
authorized, under the jurisdiction of the Health andHuman Services
Department, for the programs established within thisSection,
$11,000,000,000 for combined Fiscal Months Februaryto December
2021.” and replacing such text in lieu with
“APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be appropriated, underthe
jurisdiction of the Health and Human Services Department,for the
programs established within this Section, $11,000,000,000for combined
Fiscal Months February to December 2021.”
(iii) in Section 8 by striking “APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be
authorized, under the jurisdiction of the Health andHuman Services
Department, for the programs established within thisSection,
$100,000,000 for combined Fiscal Months February toDecember
2021.” and replacing such text in lieu with
“APPROPRIATIONS — There shall be appropriated, underthe
jurisdiction of the Health and Human Services Department,for the
programs established within this Section, $100,000,000for combined
Fiscal Months February to December 2021.”
SECTION 4. EFFECTIVE DATE.
EFFECTIVE DATE.—The provisions of this Act shall apply immediately upon passage.
