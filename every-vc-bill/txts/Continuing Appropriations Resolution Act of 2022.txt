IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

Mr.Holland (forhimself)introduced thefollowing bill;
A JOINT RESOLUTION
Toreopenthegovernmentandcontinueappropriations until anewConsolidated
Appropriationsispresented.
Be it resolved by the House of Representatives and Senate of the United States
of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION1.SHORTTITLE.
This Act may be cited as the “Continuing Appropriations Resolution of
September 2022.”
SEC.2.DEFINITIONS.
(a) DEFINITIONS.— In context of this Act, Congress defines —
(i) “department” as any federal agency, board, department or other
government entity
SEC.3.PROVIDINGFORCONTINUINGAPPROPRIATIONS.
An amount equal to half of expenditures, rounded to the nearest dollar, of the
applicable appropriations outlined in the FUNDING Act of 2022 shall be
appropriated for the several departments, agencies, corporations, and other
organizational units of the government to continue any projects or activities that
were conducted in the fiscal months of May to June, 2022. The appropriations

authorized by this Act shall be available until thirty days after this Act comes into
force.
