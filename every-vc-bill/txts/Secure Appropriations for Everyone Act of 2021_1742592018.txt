A BILL
To appropriate money in case of a government shutdown in November
of 2021.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in
Congress Assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
(a) SHORT TITLE.—This Act may be cited as—
(1) the “Secure Appropriations for Everyone Act of November 2021”, or;
(2) the “S.A.F.E. Act November 2021”.
SEC. 2 FINDINGS
(a) Congress finds that a government shutdown is a very real threat due to
the debate and reconciliation process of budget-writing and amending.

SEC. 3 CONTINUED APPROPRIATIONS
(a) IN GENERAL.—There are appropriated monies, in such ratios as
prescribed by the October Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, for one-hundred
forty-four hours past midnight on November 5th, 2021, to prevent a government
shutdown.
(b) FUNDING.—The amounts appropriated for one-hundred forty-four hours
shall be $74,920,445,438.40, to be apportioned per Subsection (c).
(c) RATIOS.—The monies appropriated above shall be appropriated to each
agency and entity by a ratio of up to but no more than 16.67 percent of those funds
given in the October Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021.
(d) VOIDING.—This act shall be repealed if either—
(1) the calendar date in Washington, D.C. is November 11th; or
(2) an appropriations act is passed.
SEC. 4 SEVERABILITY
(a) SEVERABILITY.—Should any provision of this Act be deemed invalid or
unconstitutional for any reason in a court with relevant jurisdiction, the rest of this
Act, and the application of the remaining provisions, shall not be affected
SEC. 5 ENACTMENT.
EFFECTIVE DATE.—This Act shall go into effect immediately upon its passage by
Congress and signature by the President.
