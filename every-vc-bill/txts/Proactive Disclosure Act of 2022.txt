SEC.2.PURPOSE
This billisdesignedto ensurethe publichas greateraccessto informationheldbythe federal

SEC.3..DEFINITIONS
For thepurposesof this bill,the following termsshallhave thefollowing meanings:

(1) “Proactive Disclosure” shallmean thepracticeof makinginformation availableto the

(2) “Information” shallmean all records,documents, data,and materialsheld bythefederal
