A BILL
To appropriate money in case of a government shutdown in March.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE
(a) SHORT TITLE — This Act may be cited as —
(i) the “Secure Appropriations For Everyone Act of March 2021”.
(ii) the “S.A.F.E. Act of March 2021”
SECTION 2. SEVERABILITY
(a) SEVERABILITY — Should any provision of this Act be deemed
invalid or unconstitutional for any reason in a court with relevant
jurisdiction, the rest of the Act, and the application of the
remaining provisions, shall not be affected.

Page 2
SECTION 3. FINDINGS
(a) FINDINGS — Congress finds that the government shutdown is a
very real threat due to the debate and reconciliation process of
budget-writing and amending.
SECTION 4. MAIN SECTION
(a) IN GENERAL — There are appropriated, monies, in such ratios as
prescribed by the Consolidated Appropriations and Technical
Amendments Act of February 2021, for ninety-six hours past
midnight on March 1st, 2020, to prevent a government shutdown.
(b) FUNDING — The amounts appropriated for seventy-two hours
shall be $55,426,592,224.90, to be apportioned per Subsection (c).
(c) RATIOS — The monies appropriated above shall be appropriated to
each agency and entity by ratio of up to but no more than 14.28
percent of those funds given in the Consolidated Appropriations
and Technical Amendments Act of February 2021.
(d) VOIDING — This act shall be repealed if either—
(i) The calendar date in Washington, D.C. is March 4th.
(ii) An appropriations act is passed.
SECTION 5. ENACTMENT
(a) ENACTMENT — The provisions of this Act shall come into force on
February 28th, 2021.
