IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
January 12, 2021
Mr. NOAH (for himself, Mr. KYPELLON, Mr. LONG, Mr. SETH, Mr. GALT,
Mr. LEMAIRE, Mr. SMITH, Mr. KANZLER, and Mr. JAMES) introduced the
following bill;
To require the Comptroller General of the United States to require a complete audit of the Federal
Reserve system and the banks of the Federal Reserve
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the “Audit the Federal Reserve Act of 2021”
SEC. 2. FINDINGS.
Congress finds the following:
(1) Congress has authority over the legislative activities of the Federal Government.
(2) An audit will increase transparency, accountability, and Congressional oversight of the
Federal Reserve.

SEC. 3. AUDIT OF THE FEDERAL RESERVE.
(a) AUDIT.—Notwithstanding 31 U.S. Code § 714, or any other provision of law, the
Comptroller General of the United States shall conduct an audit of the Board of Governors of the
Federal Reserve System and the Federal reserve banks of that system, as provided by Subsection b
of 31 U.S. Code § 714, within 90 days of the passage of this Act.
(b) REPORT.—Within 30 days of the passage of this Act, the Comptroller General of the
United States shall submit a report to Congress, which shall be published and made part of the
publicly-accessible record, detailing this audit.
(i) CONTENTS.—
(1) This report shall contain thorough descriptions of all findings, as well as
the conclusion of the Comptroller General.
(2) This report shall also contain recommendations of the Comptroller
General for legislative or administrative action on any findings or
conclusion contained in the audit.
SEC. 4. TIMELINE.
This Act shall enter into effect immediately after its passage.
SEC. 5. SEVERABILITY.
The provisions of this Act are severable; should any provision of this Act be found
unconstitutional or unenforceable, or be otherwise stricken, the remainder of this Act shall remain in
full force and effect.
