A BILL
To provide funding to the City of Springfield, Illinois, for the repair of the Main
Street Bridge.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. Short Title
(a) This Act may be cited as the ‘Springfield Main Street Bridge Funding Act of
2024.”
SEC. 2. Main Street Bridgen Grant
(a) There is appropriated through a block grant, through the United States
Department of Transportation, to the City of Springfield, Illinois, Lincoln, to
the sum of $5 million dollars to aid in the repair of the Main Street Bridge in
Springfield.
