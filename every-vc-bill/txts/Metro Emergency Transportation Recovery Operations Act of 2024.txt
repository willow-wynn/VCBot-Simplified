A BILL
To provide funds for the repair of the DC Metro.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States
of America in Congress assembled that the following sums are appropriated,
out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the
Department of Transportation, and related agencies, to remain available until
expended:,
SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE
This Act may be cited as the "Metro Emergency Transporation Recovery
Operations Act” or the “M.E.T.R.O” Act.
SECTION 2. FINDINGS
Congress finds the following:
(1) It is necessary to repair the District of Columbia Metro System
after the recent attack carried out by terrorist organization Pax Mortis.
SECTION 3. APPROPRIATION FOR REPAIR

There is appropriated through block grant by the United States Department
of Transportation, to the City of Washington, D.C. the sum of four billion
dollars to aid in the repair of the Washington D.C. Metro System.
SECTION 4. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this act shall be held or made invalid by a court decision,
statute or rule, or shall be otherwise rendered invalid, the remainder of this
act shall not be affected thereby.
SECTION 5. ENACTMENT
The bill shall come into effect immediately after its signature by the
President of the United States.
