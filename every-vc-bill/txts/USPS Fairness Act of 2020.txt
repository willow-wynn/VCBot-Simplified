H. R. 30330
To amend title 5, United States Code, to repeal the requirement that the United States Postal
Service prepay future retirement benefits, and for other purposes.
——————————————————————————————————
IN THE CONGRESS OF THE UNITED STATES
May 22, 2020
Mr. Archer of Massachusetts (for himself, ) proposed the following bill.
——————————————————————————————————
Sec.1. Short title
1. This Act may be cited as the "USPS Fairness Act".
Section.2. Repeal of required prepayment of future Postal Service retirement
benefits
1. Subsection (d) of section 8909a of title 5, United States Code, is repealed.
