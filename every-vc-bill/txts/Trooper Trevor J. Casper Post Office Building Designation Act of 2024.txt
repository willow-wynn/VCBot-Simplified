173rd CONGRESS
S.6
IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
JANUARY 24, 2024
Introduced by Sen. Pluribus
A BILL
To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located at 220 Fremont
Street in Kiel, Wisconsin, Lincoln, as the “Trooper Trevor J. Casper Post Office
Building”.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of
America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. TROOPER TREVOR J. CASPER POST OFFICE BUILDING.
(a) DESIGNATION.—The facility of the United States Postal Service located at
220 Fremont Street in Kiel,Wisconsin, Lincoln, shall be known and designated
as the “Trooper Trevor J. Casper Post Office Building”.
(b) REFERENCES.—Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper,
or other record of the United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a)
shall be deemed to be a reference to the “Trooper Trevor J. Casper Post Office
Building”.
