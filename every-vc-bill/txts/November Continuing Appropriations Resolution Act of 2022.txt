Be it resolved by the House of Representatives and Senate of the United States

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
This Act may be cited as the “November Continuing Appropriations
Resolution.”
SEC. 2. DEFINITIONS.
(a) DEFINITIONS.— In context of this Act, Congress defines —
(i) “department” as any federal agency, board, department or other
government entity
SEC. 3. PROVIDING FOR CONTINUING APPROPRIATIONS.
(a) An amount equal to half of expenditures, rounded to the nearest dollar, of the
applicable appropriations outlined in the FUNDING Act of 2022 shall be
appropriated for the several departments, agencies, corporations, and other
organizational units of the government to continue any projects or activities
that were conducted in the fiscal months of May to June, 2022.

SEC. 4. ENACTMENT.
(a) The appropriations authorized by this Act shall be available until thirty days
after this Act comes into force.
